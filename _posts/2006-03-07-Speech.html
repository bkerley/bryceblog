It’s hard staying on topic when you’re just so excited about an upcoming trip during spring break. The WCSC group is taking a trip to Georgia this weekend for a computer security competition, and I’m not even sure where to get started in preparing for it, because it’s all IT security and not actually an attack/defense exercise.

It’s going to be an interesting year as ACM president.
