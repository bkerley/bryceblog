The IKEA stuff I bought was bought on my own budget.

  1. DON’T GO ON THE WEEKEND
  2. SERIOUSLY, GO DURING THE WEEK
  3. Shop ahead of time and put the shopping list of the stuff you want on your phone or something (use dropbox)
  4. Rent a minivan if you’re buying anything long or wide like a bed or desk
  5. Eat right before you go in, or eat there
  6. Buy your stuff and get out
